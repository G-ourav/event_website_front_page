Food Stalls: A Culinary Adventure

Our carnival food stalls are a feast for the senses. We bring together a diverse range of food vendors, each offering mouthwatering delicacies that cater to every palate. Here are some of the culinary highlights you can expect at our festival

नैन्सी सुपर सोफ्टी (Nancy Super Softy): Indulge in the creamy and delightful super softy ice creams that promise to cool you down on a hot day. Known for their quality and hygiene, Nancy Super Softy offers a variety of flavors that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Khusbhu Super Softy: Another fantastic stop for softy lovers, Khusbhu Super Softy provides a hygienic and quality food experience. Enjoy their delicious range of softy ice creams in a clean and inviting environment.

Kajla Madras Cafe: Savor the very best flavors from around the country with Kajla Madras Cafe. Specializing in South Indian breakfast food, they bring the authentic taste of the South to your plate with dishes like dosas, idlis, and vadas, all made with love and tradition.

Brijwasi Mathura Vaale: For those craving traditional sweets and snacks from Mathura, Brijwasi Mathura Vaale is a must-visit. Known for their delectable sweets, they offer a taste of authentic Mathura delicacies that will transport you to the heart of Uttar Pradesh.

Amily Restaurant: Experience a culinary journey with Amily Restaurant, offering a variety of dishes that cater to all tastes. From local favorites to international cuisines, their menu is designed to provide a memorable dining experience for every visitor.

Safety Services: Your Well-being is Our Priority

At Mella, the safety and well-being of our guests are our top priorities. We have comprehensive safety measures in place to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience for everyone:

Ambulance Services: We have a dedicated medical team and ambulances on standby to provide immediate assistance in case of any health emergencies. Our trained professionals are equipped to handle a variety of situations, ensuring prompt and efficient care.

Fire Brigade: Safety is paramount, and we are prepared for any eventuality. Our on-site fire brigade is ready to respond swiftly to any fire-related emergencies, ensuring the safety of all attendees.

Security Personnel: Our team of security personnel is present throughout the carnival to maintain order and assist guests as needed. They are trained to handle crowd control, lost and found services, and other security concerns.

Local Police Collaboration: We work closely with the local police to ensure the highest level of security at our events. Their presence and cooperation help us maintain a safe environment for all our guests, providing peace of mind and ensuring that everyone can enjoy the festivities without worry.
